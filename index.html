Personal Description

Where I'm from:

I was born and raised in Pontotoc, Mississippi. I've lived here all my life and I couldn't have imagined growing up anywhere else. Pontotoc has been recognized numerous times for our having such a great education throughoutour school district. The town of Pontotoc isn't small nor entirely enourmous. It is just the perfect size to the point where everyone knows everyone and we're all, basically, one large, happy family.

Education info:

I am a 2017 graduate from Pontotoc High. Two weeks before I graduated, I had no idea what I was going to do or where I was going to go. Luckily, I heard of a computer software engineer school that had just started up in 2016 called Base Camp. I am currently enrolled at BCCA (Base Camp Coding Academy) and I couldn't be any more satisfied.
