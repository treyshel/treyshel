Where I'm from:

I was born and raised in Pontotc, Mississippi. I've lived here all my life and I couldn't have imagined growing up anywhere else. Pontotoc has been recognized numerous times for our having such a great education throughout our school district. The town of Pontotoc isn't small nor entirely enourmous. It is just the perfect size to the point where everyone knows everyone and we all get along.
